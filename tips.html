    Locations and Places

    Here are some of our top tips for finding the perfect accomodation, locations, tourist sites and more, when safety is a priority:


    What are the ammenities?


    This might sound simple, but whatever type of accomodation you plan on staying in, look at what ammenities are available. The accomondations website, or external site like booking.com, will give you a list of amenities, so you can check if your hotel, hostel, motel or bnb have things like: 24 hour security, a safe in your room, a taxi service trusted by your accomodation, on site parking and more. It should also be able to tell you how far your accomodation is from tourist attractions, so you can plan out travel routes.


    What are the reviews like?


    Make sure to take a look at the reviews of the accomodation you're staying in, some sites, like Tripadvisor will let you refine your search so you can take a look at reviews from other solo travelers and find out whether the accomodation is right for you.
